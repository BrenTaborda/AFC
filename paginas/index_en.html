About Us

We are a group of family businesses specialized in the production of wines from different terrains in Mendoza, Argentina. We own vineyards and wineries and we work every day with the commitment to create high quality wines, while preserving the characteristics of the soils that gave them origin. In collaboration with our partners, we develop our brands at regional and/or global level, and we seek to provide an excellent service to our customers in Argentina and around the world.

More information about us:

Our Vineyards

The grapes selected for the elaboration of our wines are from different producing areas of the Province of Mendoza. Some of them are: Maipú, Luján de Cuyo, San Martín, among others. The variety of soils, the unique characteristics of each vineyard and the special care in working them give rise to a great diversity of wines. The combination of the vineyard’s fruits together with the diligent performance of our winemakers, gives us the possibility to offer a wide range of totally distinctive products of excellent quality.
